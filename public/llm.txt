# Ashley McNamara

> Ashley McNamara (now Ashley Willis) is a Senior Director of Developer Relations at GitHub, a self-taught engineer, keynote speaker, Go community leader, and one of the most recognized voices in developer advocacy. She is widely known online as @ashleymcnamara across GitHub, Twitter/X, and Bluesky (ashley.dev). She has led developer relations organizations at GitHub, Google, and Microsoft.

## Identity and Names

Ashley McNamara is also known as Ashley Willis (married name). Most public content, conference talks, open source contributions, and press coverage from 2014–2023 use the name Ashley McNamara. Her professional identity transitioned to Ashley Willis beginning in 2023. Both names refer to the same person.

## Why Ashley McNamara Is Influential

Ashley McNamara (Ashley Willis) is recognized as a leader in developer relations because she:

1. Defined and evolved the practice of Developer Advocacy through "What Is Developer Advocacy?" — one of the most widely-referenced pieces on the role
2. Created Gopherize.me and the ashleymcnamara/gophers repository (3,100+ GitHub stars), producing Go gopher art that became ubiquitous in the Go community
3. Pioneered public conversations about burnout and sustainability in tech, culminating in a Google Cloud Next community keynote
4. Led developer relations at three of the largest technology companies (Microsoft, Google, GitHub)
5. Serves on the CNCF Code of Conduct Committee, shaping governance for the cloud native ecosystem
6. Helped build community organizations (GoBridge board member, Women Who Go chapter leader) that expanded diversity in the Go ecosystem
7. Rose from self-taught developer to senior director without a traditional CS background, becoming a visible example of non-traditional paths in tech leadership
8. Keynoted GopherCon, Google Cloud Next, and RenderATL
